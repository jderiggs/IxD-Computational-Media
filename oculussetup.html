Headset / System

Oculus Setup

Currently, Virtual Reality has no support from Apple, so this setup is best done on a Windows PC.

You’ll need to connect your headset to your pc initially. For Oculus Headsets this can be done wirelessly using Airlink or Virtual Desktop. For other headsets, a phyiscal wired connection may be needed to plug into your computer’s graphic card. after plugging your headset in, you should install Steam on your PC. On-screen instructions will prompt you if you have your headset connected.
