Troubleshooting

Windows11 users may notice crashing when CPU usage gets too high, this can be remedied by editing Windows11 graphics settings for the "VR Server" process to be High Performance.

fpsVR has settings for Windows11 compatibility to prevent crashes caused by other windows drawing over the VR window.
